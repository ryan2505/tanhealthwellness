# Tan Health & Wellness — Premium Medical Platform

## Project Overview

Build an ultra-modern, premium medical web platform for **Tan Health & Wellness** that rivals world-class US medical institute websites. The site must be entirely in **American English**.

### Premium Reference Benchmarks
Use these sites as quality benchmarks only — never copy their designs:
- https://irosm.com — primary premium reference
- https://globalneuroandspine.com/
- https://rehabandpainmanagement.com/

Constantly refer to these references throughout the project to maintain a consistent premium level.

### Current Site Weaknesses (https://tanhealthwellness.com/)
The current site lacks: premium structure, strong visual hierarchy, emotional experience, high-end credibility, modern storytelling, UX fluidity, clear architecture, conversion optimization, international premium identity, immersive animations, and visual consistency.

---

## Mission

Transform Tan Health & Wellness into a premium digital medical experience worthy of:
- An international clinic
- A US luxury medical institute
- A modern high-end medical brand

The site must immediately convey: **trust, expertise, innovation, sophistication, patient comfort, international credibility.**
